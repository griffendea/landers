3 QUALIFICATION CRITERIA

What you need to know to get approved for the 2026 Debt Relief Program

This program isn't open to everyone. Review the requirements below — the last one is the one people miss.

1. Total debt of $25,000 or more

Programs are built around meaningful debt loads. If your combined balances are under $25,000, you likely won't qualify for this particular program.

2. Debt must be unsecured

Credit cards, medical bills, personal loans, and collections generally qualify. Mortgages, auto loans, and other secured debt do not.

3. You must answer the phone

This is the step most people get wrong. Once you submit your information, a specialist will call you — call volume for this program is high, and if you miss the call, it can be very hard to get reconnected. Keep your phone nearby and answer any call from an unfamiliar number over the next few minutes.

Takes about 60 seconds. No obligation to enroll.
